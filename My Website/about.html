Welcome!

Are you new to volleyball? Or are you an experienced player looking for a team that will allow you to further improve your skills? Well it doesn't matter, because UCD Volleball Club is for everyone!

Volleyball is one of the fastest growing sports in Ireland, so why don't you give it a try in one of the best college team in the country!

During 2017/2018 Academic year our team participated in many different tournaments in which we always achieved good results. We finished the year by getting the second place in All-Ireland Volleyball College League.

There are few reasons why are team is at the top for so many years. We treat everyone in the team as part of our Volleyball family. We also have great staff of dedicated people and our coaches will make sure You achieve your full potential.

If you are completely new to volleyball and You aren't sure whether this is something for You then make sure why to visit the Guide Section where we will introduce You to basic rules of volleyball.

Or maybe you are someone who already knows how to play and You are thinking whether to join. Don't hesitate! Even if You aren't fully convinced that UCD Volleyball Club is a place for you then why don't You come to one of our trainings so You can see for yourself? Go to the Contact Section to find out when and where our next training is taking place.
