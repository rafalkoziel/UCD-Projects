What is volleyball?

This is only a very basic introduction to volleyball to give You a general idea about volleyball. If you already know basics then join us during one of the training sessions to learn more and improve your skills ;) .

So volleyball is a team sport in which two teams are separated by a net. Each team can score a point by grounding a ball on the opposing side of the net, given that no rules were broken. Each team consists of 6 players and a libero. Each match is played in either best of 5 or best of 3 formula. Each set is played up to 25 points where a team has to win with 2 points difference.

Each action starts by a player serving a ball over a net. The opposing team can touch the ball only 3 times and each player can only touch it once. Ideal action consists of pass (recieving the ball),set (setting the ball to person who is attacking) and a hit. If ball touches the ground the team who hit the ball gets a point.

Well that's pretty much all you need to know to start playing!
